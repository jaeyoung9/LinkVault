Payment & Refund Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Ad-Free Pass

  • 7-Day Pass: Removes all ads for 7 days from the time of purchase.
  • 30-Day Pass: Removes all ads for 30 days from the time of purchase.
  • All core features remain fully available regardless of pass status.
  • The system prevents purchasing a new pass while an active pass exists.

Donations

  • One-time: Suggested amounts of $5, $10, $25, $50 (custom amount also available).
  • Recurring: Suggested monthly amounts of $3, $5, $10 (custom amount also available). Cancel anytime from your Settings page.
  • Supporter badge is awarded after any completed donation and persists even after cancellation.
  • No features are gated behind supporter status — the badge is symbolic.

Refund Eligibility

  • Ad-Free Pass: Full refund within 24 hours. After that, case-by-case for technical issues.
  • One-time donations: Refundable within 7 days.
  • Recurring donations: Cancel anytime; no partial-month refund, but benefits continue through the billing period.
  • Duplicate charges are automatically detected. Contact support if you believe you were charged twice.

Failed Payments

  • No charge is applied on payment failure. No pass or donation is recorded until payment is confirmed.
  • For recurring subscriptions, failed payments are retried up to 3 times over 7 days before cancellation.
  • You will receive an email notification if a recurring payment fails.

Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely through Stripe. LinkVault does not store your card details. For payment-related issues, contact us through the support channels in your Settings page. Include your username and the approximate date of the transaction. We aim to respond within 3-5 business days.
