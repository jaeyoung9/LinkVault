Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Ad-Free Pass Refunds

  • You may request a full refund within 24 hours of purchasing a 7-day or 30-day Ad-Free Pass.
  • After 24 hours, refunds are issued on a case-by-case basis for technical issues or accidental purchases.
  • If you experience a malfunction where ads continue to display despite an active pass, contact support for a resolution or refund.
  • Duplicate charges are automatically detected. If you believe you were charged twice, contact us and we will investigate and refund promptly.

Donation Refunds

  • One-time donations can be refunded within 7 days of the transaction.
  • Recurring donations can be cancelled at any time from your Settings page. No further charges will be applied after cancellation.
  • Partial-month refunds for recurring donations are not provided, but you will retain access to supporter benefits through the end of the current billing period.

Failed Payments

  • If a payment fails, no charge is applied and no pass or donation is recorded.
  • For recurring subscriptions, Stripe will retry failed payments up to 3 times over 7 days before cancelling the subscription.
  • You will receive an email notification if a recurring payment fails.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us through the support channels available in your Settings page. Include your username and the approximate date of the transaction. We aim to process refund requests within 3-5 business days.
