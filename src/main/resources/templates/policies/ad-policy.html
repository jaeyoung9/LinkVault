Advertising Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Our Approach to Ads

LinkVault uses a voluntary ad model. Advertising helps fund the service, but we believe you should always be in control. All core features work the same whether or not you see ads.

Ad Identification

Every advertisement on LinkVault is clearly labeled with a "Sponsored" badge. We never disguise ads as organic content. Ad cards are styled to match the overall look of the feed while maintaining a distinct identification label.

Ad Frequency Limits

  • Maximum of 3 ad cards per page load
  • Maximum of 10 ads per session
  • Maximum of 15 ads per hour
  • Ads appear at regular intervals (approximately 1 per every 6 posts)
  • New visitors enjoy a grace period with reduced ad frequency

Your Control

  • You can hide any ad by clicking the "Hide" button and selecting a reason
  • Hidden ads will not reappear in your session
  • If you prefer an ad-free experience, you can choose a 7-day or 30-day Ad-Free Pass
  • You can also earn ad-free time by watching optional reward videos

Privacy & Targeting

Rewarded Videos

Reward videos are entirely opt-in. You choose when to watch, and we clearly inform you of the reward before you start. You are never required to watch a video to use any feature of LinkVault.
